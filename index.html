Birth Record Monitoring System

What is Birth Record Monitoring?

Birth record monitoring is the continuous tracking and updating of birth registration data. It ensures timely registration, vaccination tracking, and access to legal documents. Our system helps ensure no newborn is left unregistered or unmonitored.

Why Birth Registration Matters

Birth registration provides a child with a legal identity, ensures access to essential services like healthcare and education, and helps governments plan for the future effectively.
